FAQ

How do daily payouts work?
Mining rewards are calculated every 24 hours based on your hashrate contribution. Earnings are automatically credited at midnight UTC. Withdraw anytime your balance exceeds the minimum threshold of 0.0645 BTC.
What is the minimum withdrawal?
Minimum withdrawal is 0.0645 BTC or equivalent. Processed within 1–3 hours. Enterprise plan users enjoy instant withdrawals with no minimum.
Can I switch between cryptocurrencies?
Yes! Pro and Enterprise plan users can switch mining power to any supported cryptocurrency in real time. Free plan users mine BTC and ETH only.
Is my account secure?
Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption, optional 2FA, and cold storage for all user funds. Regular third-party security audits are conducted. Enable 2FA in Settings for maximum protection.
How do I contact technical support?
Email our support team at genesisminingcompanygmc19@gmail.com. You can also use the Live Chat button above or the chatbot in the bottom-right corner. Average response time is under 2 minutes for live chat.
